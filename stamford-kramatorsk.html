🇺🇦 Kramatorsk, Ukraine

Population: ~150,000
Region: Donetsk Oblast
Founded: 1868

Kramatorsk is a major industrial city in eastern Ukraine, known for its machine-building industry and rich cultural heritage. Despite facing significant challenges, the city remains resilient and continues to support its residents through difficult times.

🇺🇸 Stamford, Connecticut

Population: ~135,000
State: Connecticut
Founded: 1641

Stamford is a thriving city on Connecticut's Gold Coast, home to numerous Fortune 500 companies and a vibrant, diverse community. The city has embraced its role as a sister city to Kramatorsk with enthusiasm and generosity.

Our Partnership Story

The partnership between Stamford and Kramatorsk began in May 2023, born from a desire to provide direct, meaningful support to a Ukrainian community in need. What started as a humanitarian initiative has blossomed into a deep friendship between two cities separated by an ocean but united in spirit.

Stamford residents, businesses, and civic organizations have rallied together to support their sister city through fundraising events, supply drives, and advocacy. The connection has brought both communities closer, fostering cultural exchange and mutual understanding.
