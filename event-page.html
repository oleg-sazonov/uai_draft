Stamford Spring Benefit Night

A community fundraiser bringing together local partners, volunteers, and supporters to raise direct aid for families in Ukraine.

Stamford Spring Benefit Night

📅 April 18 – 20, 2026 📍 Stamford Center for Community Arts, Stamford, CT

Join neighbors, civic leaders, and volunteers for an evening focused on practical support, long-term partnership, and direct humanitarian aid.

Event Overview

This event supports Ukraine Aid International's ongoing partnership work and emergency response efforts. Guests will hear field updates, meet local organizers, and learn how community-led fundraising creates lasting impact.

What to Expect

  • Mission update from recent aid deliveries
  • Community speaker panel and partner remarks
  • Volunteer sign-up opportunities
  • Fundraising appeal and donor recognition

Program Highlights

"Every local event creates real momentum for the communities we serve in Ukraine."

— Ukraine Aid International team

Doors open at 6:00 PM each evening. The main program begins at 6:30 PM, followed by a donor and volunteer information session.
